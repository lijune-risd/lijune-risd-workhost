Definition
김밥 | 설명
Gimbap or "김밥" is similar to the Japanese mari. Like sushi, Gimbap has sorts of variations that you can customize to your own good. From cooked sauted meat to Carrots, Korean root vegetables, and Cucumber -- Gimbap essentially holds together a customizable experience for your to curate. Every Korean has their own personal Gimbap experience tailored based on their surroundings. The website aims to make you create your own "Gimbap" as well.
